Meet Our Pastors Who Share God’s Love

Rev Charles Kasau
Reverend Charles Kasau is a dedicated pastor who plays a significant role in his church community. Known for his compassionate leadership and strong sense of spiritual guidance, he actively engages with members of his congregation, offering support and mentorship. Reverend Kasau collaborates with key figures like Lydia Ngui and Peter Muli, working together to foster a welcoming and nurturing environment within the church. His commitment to spreading faith and providing moral counsel has made him a respected figure, admired for his wisdom and dedication to service.
Pastor Lydia Ngui
Pastor Lydia Ngui is a dynamic and compassionate leader within her church community, known for her deep commitment to spiritual growth and outreach. With a passion for empowering others, she actively engages in various ministry activities, offering guidance and support to congregation members. Lydia's ability to connect with people from diverse backgrounds has made her a trusted mentor and friend to many. Her collaborative work with fellow leaders, including Reverend Charles Kasau and Peter Muli, reflects her dedication to fostering a united and vibrant church environment. Through her sermons and community initiatives, Pastor Lydia inspires others to deepen their faith and live out their beliefs in meaningful ways.
Pastor Peter Muli
Pastor Peter Muli is a devoted and influential figure within his church community, recognized for his commitment to service and spiritual leadership. Known for his approachable demeanor and insightful teachings, he plays a vital role in guiding congregation members on their faith journeys. Pastor Muli actively participates in community outreach initiatives, aiming to make a positive impact both within the church and the wider community. His collaborative efforts with fellow pastors, including Reverend Charles Kasau and Pastor Lydia Ngui, showcase his dedication to fostering unity and growth among church members. Through his sermons and personal interactions, Pastor Peter inspires others to embrace their faith and live with purpose, making him a respected and beloved leader in the congregation.
I’m New Here
Welcome! Starting something new can feel overwhelming, but remember that every journey begins with a single step. Here, you’ll find a supportive community ready to help you grow and thrive. Embrace this opportunity to learn and connect, and don't hesitate to reach out for guidance or friendship. Every person you meet was once new, just like you, and together we can create a space filled with encouragement and hope. Trust in the process, and know that brighter days are ahead as you embark on this exciting chapter!!!
Care Ministries
Care ministries are a vital part of the church's mission, focusing on providing support and compassion to those in need. These ministries offer services like counseling, visitation for the sick or elderly, and meal delivery for families facing hardships. By reaching out to individuals and families, care ministries foster a sense of community and belonging, promoting healing and hope while reminding everyone that they are never alone on their journey.
Prayer Request
Prayer requests are an important aspect of community support, allowing individuals to share their needs and concerns with others. They create a space for collective prayer, fostering connection and encouragement among members. By lifting each other up in prayer, we strengthen our bonds and provide comfort during challenging times, reminding everyone that they are not alone in their struggles and that hope is always within reach.
Audio Podcasts
Audio podcasts are a versatile medium for sharing stories and insights, covering diverse topics from personal development to entertainment. Their conversational format fosters connection between hosts and listeners, making them accessible and engaging. Whether for inspiration or information, podcasts offer a unique way to explore new ideas anytime and anywhere.
